Don't just visit for the grub! Each month (usually the last Monday), we will select a local charity or non-profit organization. Baileys’ Range will donate 25% of ALL SALES from 5-8pm to the selected charity. Please join us for Community Table Night at Range and help support some of St. Louis’ amazing local organizations!
