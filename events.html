Community Table Night

Community Table Night at Baileys’ Range is an event about St. Louis, for the benefit of St. Louis, because we love St. Louis! We want to give back to the people of the city that have given us so much. Each month (usually the last Monday), we will select a local charity or non-profit organization. Baileys’ Range will donate 25% of ALL SALES from 5-8pm to the selected charity. Please join us for Community Table Night at Range and help support some of St. Louis’ amazing local organizations!

If you have or know of a local organization that would like to participate in Community Table Night, please click here to submit your information.
